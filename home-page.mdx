### Frontegg Solutions

#### Authentication

Prevent unauthorized access by unauthorized users or machines. 

[](https://docs.frontegg.com/docs/entitlements) 

#### Entitlements

Control your users' access to specific features or functionalities based on their designated user entitlements. 

[](https://docs.frontegg.com/docs/security) 

#### Security

Enhance your application security by activating Frontegg's robust security features

[](https://docs.frontegg.com/docs/admin-portal-introduction) 

#### Admin Portal

Control, manage, fortify, and customize your offering and your client's experience while using your app

[](https://docs.frontegg.com/docs/sub-account-management) 

#### Hierarchies

Give your accounts the ability to create and manage sub-accounts in a hierarchy

#### Backoffice and User Management

Manage accounts and user and control user activity in your app
