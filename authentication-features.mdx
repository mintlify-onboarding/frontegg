Frontegg offers a versatile range of authentication capabilities to ensure the security and flexibility of your application's login process. Users can choose their preferred authentication methods, making the login experience tailored to their preferences. These capabilities include traditional password and modern passwordless authentication, as well as passkeys for quick and secure access. Explore our various authentication methods via the quick links below:
